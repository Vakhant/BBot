Problem

Ad fraud refers to fake clicks, visits, and user actions that mimic real behavior but deliver zero business value.

The most common sources are:
• automated bots,
• competitors clicking ads,
• click farms and proxy traffic.

The real danger? It looks like normal traffic in reports, yet it can silently consume 30–50% of your budget, distort your analytics, damage behavioral metrics, and block proper campaign optimization.

The result — you’re paying for visibility, not conversions.
